My Favorite Food

My favorite food is Zereshk Polo. In English, it can be translated as Barberry Rice with chicken. I like it to be served with Shirazi salad. This dish is a staple in Persian cuisine, known for its unique combination of tangy barberries and savory chicken, all served on a bed of fluffy saffron rice.
